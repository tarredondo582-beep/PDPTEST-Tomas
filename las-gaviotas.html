Subdivision FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Restriction details are specific to this subdivision and should be verified with the office and proper authorities before purchase.

Are Mobile Homes Allowed?

Yes, Mobile homes may be permitted with developer approval. Please ensure you also check with the local Hidalgo County Precinct for any additional approvals required. Only 1 single family home allowed per lot.

What Are The Restrictions On Keeping Animals?

Livestock, including horses, cattle, poultry, pigs, and other barnyard animals, are not allowed, with the exception of small barnyard fowl such as chickens or guinea hens, which must be completely penned. Only house pets are allowed to roam the property, but they must be leashed or confined when outside.

What Are The Setback Requirements For Building?

The minimum building setback requirements for Las Gaviotas Subdivision are as follows: Front Setback: 25 Feet. Rear Setback: 15 Feet. Side Setback: 6 Feet. Corner Side Setback: 10 Feet. Corner: 20 Feet. Easements or right-of-way (ROW) requirements will take precedence if they are greater.

Can I Park A commercial vehicle Or An 18-wheeler On The Property?

NO, tractor-trailer rigs or 18-wheelers are not allowed to be parked on any street in the subdivision, except for the delivery or pickup of household items.

What Are The Allowed Uses For Lots In The subdivision?

Lots are restricted to permanent single-family residential use only, unless otherwise approved by the necessary authorities and the developer.
