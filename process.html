Common buyer concerns

Simple answers before they call.

Can I rely on the website availability?

Use it as a guide, but call the office to confirm current price, terms, and availability before making plans.

Should I start with a subdivision page or lot finder?

On mobile, start with Available Lots. Once you find a match, open the subdivision page for the plat map, FAQ, and contact options.

What should I ask when I call?

Ask about the lot number, current price, down payment, estimated monthly payment, restrictions, utilities, and closing process.

Ready to narrow it down?

Find two or three lots you like, then call to verify.

This keeps the process simple and avoids wasting time on sold or reserved lots.
