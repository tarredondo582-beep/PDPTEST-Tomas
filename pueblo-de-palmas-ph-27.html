Subdivision FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Restriction details are specific to this subdivision and should be verified with the office and proper authorities before purchase.

Are Mobile Homes Allowed?

Yes, Mobile homes may be permitted with developer approval. Please ensure you also check with the local Hidalgo County Precinct for any additional approvals required. Only 1 single family home allowed per lot.

What Are The Restrictions On Keeping Animals?

Horses, cattle, pigs, and hogs are not prohibited on any lot. Poultry, goats, sheep, and household pets are allowed, provided a suitable fence is constructed to contain them. No livestock or house pets shall be tethered in the front yard or on any unfenced lot.

What Are The Setback Requirements For Building?

The minimum building setback requirements for Milanos Estates PH I are as follows: Front Setback: 20.00 feet or easement, whichever is greater. Rear Setback: 10.00 feet or easement, whichever is greater. Side Setback: 6.00 feet or easement, whichever is greater. Corner Side Setback: 10.00 feet or easement, whichever is greater. Easements or right-of-way (ROW) requirements will take precedence if they are greater.

Can I Park A commercial vehicle Or An 18-wheeler On The Property?

NO, tractor-trailer rigs or 18-wheelers are not allowed to be parked on any street in the subdivision, except for the delivery or pickup of household items.

What Are The Allowed Uses For Lots In The subdivision?

Lots are generally intended for permanent single-family residential use. Buyers should confirm lot-specific uses and approvals with the office and the proper authorities.
