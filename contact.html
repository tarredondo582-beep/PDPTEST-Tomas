About Us

Pueblo de Palmas, Inc. is a family-owned, Rio Grande Valley land company specializing in owner-financed lots across Hidalgo and Starr Counties. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to own land — regardless of their credit history or bank relationship.

Licensed under NMLS #319106, we hold the note on every property we sell. No banks, no middlemen, no lengthy approval processes. When you buy from us, you're working directly with the people who own the land.

From half-acre residential lots in Donna and Edinburg to 10-acre ranch tracts in Rio Grande City, we have options for every family and budget in the Valley. Call us today and let's find your lot.
